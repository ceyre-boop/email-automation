TABOOST Email Automation

Public information page for TABOOST's Gmail automation app used by invited talent and internal managers.

This page is publicly accessible and does not require login.

What this app does

  • Reads incoming brand partnership emails from connected Gmail inboxes
  • Classifies emails and prepares draft responses for human review
  • Logs workflow metadata to internal operations systems

How onboarding works

Invited users receive a dedicated onboarding link from their manager. OAuth consent is handled by Google.
